=====Chompérs Dustweather=====
Daughter of [[Toothless]]. A native of the [[Mestra]] System, Chompérs grew up in a mining family. 
[[Poundies]]
Sometime around 1 ABY, Chompérs and her ragtag mining crew, happened upon an old asteroid that seemed to have a bare atmosphere clinging to it. Upon landing, they scouted some old ruins dating back to the time when the asteroid field was still a whole planet.
